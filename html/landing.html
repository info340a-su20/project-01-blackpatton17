The pandemic shifted school to distant learning. It benefits the students to set their own schedule. Though, some recounted the challenges of distance learning, from struggling to understanding the assignments to simply lack of opportunity to meet new friends.

We help you find your class buddies base on your preferences, and like dating sites, you will find people who are also looking for a buddy and matched with your preferences. You can chat with them and study together. You can view other students review on the classes and submit a review yourself.

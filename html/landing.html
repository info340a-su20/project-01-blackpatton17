Match. Chat. Study.

The pandemic shifted school to distant learning. It benefits the students to set their own schedule. Though, some recounted the challenges of distance learning, from struggling to understanding the assignments to simply lack of opportunity to meet new friends.

We help you find your class buddies base on your preferences, and like dating sites, you will find people who are also looking for a buddy and matched with your preferences. You can chat with them and study together.

Match

Find your study partners from your class base on your preferences.You can also post your information for others to find you.

Chat

Direct message your matched classmates and start chatting! Chat can be found under the profile panel.

Study

Set up a time to meet each other and study. Motivate and keep each other on track. Classes can be easier with a buddy.
